500 Hour Yoga Lifestyle Practicioner Teacher Training

The 500-hour Yoga Lifestyle Practitioner and Teacher Training Program is open to anyone who wants to learn in depth the teachings of Yoga with the option to become a certified Yoga Teacher. The Program is designed to deliver authentic content of Traditional Yoga as well as modern practices and approaches to give students a comprehensive understanding of Yoga through a variety of courses. At Hawaii School of Yoga we respect every style of Yoga that raises human dignity and helps people connect with Divine Source. Our role is not to define your Yoga path but to help you refine yourself as a Yogi and deepen your personal practice.
