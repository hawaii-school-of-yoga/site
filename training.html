Why Choose Hawai'i School of Yoga, Tantra and Ayurveda?

  • Authenticity - our school is founded upon the authentic gurus and lineage holders of Yoga, Tantra and Ayurveda
  • Structure - our approach is systematic and goal oriented to help you succeed in all aspects of becoming a yoga teacher
  • All Inclusive (Universal) - our comprehensive trainings incorporate all aspects and styles of yoga, we believe that yoga IS for everybody.
  • Ethics - we uphold the Yogic vows (Yamas and Niyamas) of non-harming, truthfulness, non-stealing, self-restraint, non-possesiveness, cleanliness, contentment, self-discipline, cultivation of wisdom through study, and reverence for life.
  • Ohana - we value relationship, once you are enrolled in our training, you become family for life.
