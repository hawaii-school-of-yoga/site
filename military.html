We Support Our Military

Hawaii School of Yoga is partnered with the GI Bill ® and MyCAA ® to provide tuition assistance to active service member, veterans and their beneficiaries.

Learn more!

Gi Bill ®

Hawaii School of Yoga is approved to certify qualifying veterans for GI Bill ® benefits for our 200 Hour Hatha Yoga and Tantra Teacher Training. To check your eligibility and to apply for benefits all veterans need to apply online. Once you have confirmed you are eligible for benefits, contact the Hawaii School of Yoga Director (808) 738-6924 to apply. Veterans and their beneficiaries who do not fall under Chapter 33, are required to pay their tuition up front and will be reimbursed by the VA. You can also contact the Gi Bill Student Help line at (888) 442-4551

Learn More Apply

MyCAA ®

Both of our Yoga Teacher Training programs- the 200 hour Hatha Yoga and Tantra Teacher Training and the Advanced 300 hour Yoga Teacher Training are approved for MyCAA scholarships.

MyCAA (my Career Advancement Account) is a scholarship program for military spouses that provides up to $4,000 of financial assistance to eligible military spouses who are pursuing a license, certification or Associate’s degree in a portable career field and occupation. If you are a spouse of an active military service member, you may be eligible tuition assistance through MyCAA. To learn more about this program and to see if you qualify for these benefits visit the MyCAA link.
